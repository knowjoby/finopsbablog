I'm Joby John, a Product Owner at EY working at the intersection of cloud infrastructure, FinOps, and invoice intelligence. I built this blog because most cloud cost content is written for finance teams or platform engineers — and almost none of it is written for the person who actually owns the product that generates the bill. That's where I sit, and that's the gap I'm filling. Expect plain language, concrete examples, and a PO-first lens on everything from reserved instance strategy to the anatomy of a Snowflake credit overrun.
